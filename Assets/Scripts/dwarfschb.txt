You have chosen to let your brother go to school. You walk into the metallurgy shop ready for your first day as an apprentice. Less than a minute after you arrive, your mentor walks up to the both of you. News of your choice to forgo secondary schooling has spread, albeit quietly. Your mentor offers you pay for the duration of your apprenticeship such that you may pay for schooling in a year. You work diligently in the metallurgy shop and are very well respected by the customers of all races. Almost a year later, you are proud of yourself for earning enough to attend school. However, you are now having second thoughts. You realize you could do a lot of other things with the money you have saved. You have always wanted to get out of this town and explore a little - you are itching for an adventure. Press the left arrow key to go to school or the right arrow key to go on an adventure.
